About Us

Uptowncorps UAE provides extensive heavy/light trucks transport services to different kinds of SMBs. We are professionals when it comes to providing fleet to Restaurants, Hotels, or Catering Businesses. We have an experienced team of drivers, skilled staff and management who are professionals and ensure that our customers are satisfied with our services.

Our Services

Uptowncorps offers a vast network of services in the transport industry. From providing fleet to managing the logistics with or without drivers is what makes us the market leaders in the transportation and fleet providers.

Chiller Van

Chiller van, Freezer Van and Deep Frozen van, We offer variety of such vans .These Vans are offered with a professional and dedicated support team which offers consultation and personal attention to the valued clients to build trust through transparency in our dealings.

Chiller Canter

Temperature controlled Canters are offered in case of a heavy vehicle requirement. Our air conditioning offers chiller temperature and freezer temperature as per the requirement. Canters with pre-Built compartments are available on demand. Canters are offered with professional drivers; well experienced of the routes and highly professional in customer dealings.

Cargo Van

Cargo Vans or Cargo trucks are available as per the requirement for the logistics. Customizable packages are available if the van/truck is required with or without the compartments to optimize the utilization of the vehicle.

Delivery Bike

We provide the delivery bikes along with the riders as an all inclusive packages the SMEs as per the requirement. Delivery bikes are with or without the storage box depending upon the requirement. We offer highly professional riders with complete knowledge of the routes and having a command over customer dealing.
